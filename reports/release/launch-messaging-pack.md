## 5) Claims to phrase carefully (recommended wording)
- Instead of: “Increase revenue by 15–20%.”  
  Use: **“Designed to improve pricing decisions and reduce manual guesswork.”**
- Instead of: “Reduce tickets by 40–60%.”  
  Use: **“Built to deflect repetitive tenant questions through self-service and FAQ automation.”**
- Instead of: “Bank-level security / GDPR compliant / SOC2 ready” (as hard claims).  
  Use: **“Security-focused architecture with role-based access, authentication controls, and audit-oriented design.”**
