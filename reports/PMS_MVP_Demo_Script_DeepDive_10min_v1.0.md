# PMS MVP Demo Script — Deep Dive (10-Minute Version)

**Audience:** operators, implementation leads, technical stakeholders  
**Length:** ~10 minutes

---

## 0) Framing (30 sec)

"I’ll walk through the PMS in the exact order operations teams experience it: leasing pipeline, payments, maintenance, inspections, owner visibility, and ML-readiness. I’ll call out where this reduces manual work and where controls improve trust."

---

## 1) Leasing Pipeline: Applicant to Tenant (2 min)

### Talk track
"We begin with property and unit context. The PM can move from listing-level visibility to applicant intake without leaving the platform."

"Applications are structured and reviewable. Once approved, we transition directly into lease onboarding and tenant state activation."

### Key points to emphasize
- Reduced re-entry across systems
- Clear status transitions (application → approved → leased)
- Better auditability for approval decisions

---

## 2) Payments: Real-World + Digital (2 min)

### Talk track
"On the tenant side, Pay Now enables digital collection. On the PM/Admin side, we now support manual operational realities: cash/check/money-order entries and manual charges."

"Every manual financial action has controls: reasoned reverse/void workflows, ledger updates, and reporting visibility."

### Key points to emphasize
- Handles mixed payment environments
- Improves reconciliation behavior
- Preserves audit chain (no silent mutation patterns)

---

## 3) Maintenance Workflow (1.5 min)

### Talk track
"Tenants submit requests with supporting detail and photos. PM queue triage is immediate, assignment logic is operator-friendly, and communication remains attached to the request timeline."

"That means less context loss, faster action, and cleaner service records."

### Key points to emphasize
- Faster triage cycles
- Better tenant communication continuity
- Stronger service accountability

---

## 4) Inspections + AI Assist (2 min)

### Talk track
"Inspection workflows are standardized across move-in, routine, and move-out. The platform captures structured checklist data that supports repeatable evaluations."

"AI then assists by generating estimate/work-plan suggestions. We preserve estimate history so teams can compare revisions and justify changes."

### Key points to emphasize
- Structured capture improves consistency
- AI is assistive, not opaque/autonomous
- Historical comparability supports trust and governance

---

## 5) Owner Perspective (1 min)

### Talk track
"Owners can view portfolio/property performance and maintenance context without waiting on manually assembled PM updates."

"This creates transparency while protecting PM time."

### Key points to emphasize
- Better owner confidence
- Lower PM reporting burden
- Clearer collaboration surface

---

## 6) ML Readiness and Rollout Discipline (1 min)

### Talk track
"We also run a readiness simulation layer. Instead of declaring AI value by assumption, we score data-readiness across inspection intelligence, maintenance predictive signals, and payment strategy signals."

"This keeps roadmap decisions grounded in measurable readiness."

### Key points to emphasize
- Evidence-first AI deployment
- Reduced production risk
- Clear path from data quality to model quality

---

## 7) Closing (30 sec)

"The core value is operational execution: one integrated system, practical controls, and auditability where it matters. The AI layer is additive and accountable — introduced where data and process maturity justify it."

"If useful, next step is an acceptance walk-through with your team’s live scenarios and a release-readiness checklist."
