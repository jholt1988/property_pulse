## 4) Claim Governance Rules (Mandatory)

Every external claim must be tagged internally as one of:
- **Verified**
- **Estimate**
- **Future**
- **Unverified** (do not publish)

## Hard publication rules
- No unverified numeric claims in public copy.
- No security/compliance guarantees without explicit approval.
- Every external asset should include “Last verified” date + owner.
- Distinguish clearly between **live now** vs **roadmap** features.

## 5) 12-Week Marketing Plan & Timeline

## Phase 1: Prelaunch + Remediation (Weeks 1–4)
**Goal:** close risk gaps, warm audience, prepare launch assets.

- Finalize claim register and evidence map
- Fix all placeholder links/contacts/tokens
- Produce media kit v1 (logo exports, hero, social cards)
- Prepare launch page and webinar assets
- Start soft thought-leadership cadence (no over-claims)

**Week 4 checkpoint:** GO/NO-GO for full launch push

## Phase 2: Launch Window (Weeks 5–6)
**Goal:** coordinated launch once gates are met.

- Launch page live
- LinkedIn/X coordinated launch posts
- Launch email wave
- Webinar #1 + snippets
- Sales enablement rollout

## Phase 3: Post-Launch Optimization (Weeks 7–12)
**Goal:** increase conversion efficiency and pipeline quality.

- A/B tests (page headlines, CTAs, email variants)
- Publish proof-backed use-case pages
- Scale top-performing channels biweekly
- Week 12 retro + next-quarter plan

## 7) Channel Campaign Playbook

## LinkedIn
- Cadence: 3/wk prelaunch, daily during launch week, 2–3/wk post-launch
- Content: operator pain points, workflow walkthroughs, short clips
- KPI: CTR, qualified demo requests, engagement quality

## X/Twitter
- Cadence: 1 thread/wk + short posts prelaunch; 2/day launch; daily weekdays after
- Content: build updates, short tactical threads, event reminders
- KPI: link clicks, qualified replies, webinar registrations

## Email
- Cadence: weekly prelaunch; 2 sends launch week; weekly + triggers post-launch
- Content: launch narrative, role use-cases, objections, proof
- KPI: open trend, CTR, booked-meeting conversion

## Website/SEO
- Cadence: 2 landing pages + 2 SEO pieces prelaunch; launch page on go-live; 1–2 pieces/wk after
- KPI: demo conversion rate, organic trend, page engagement

## Webinar/Video
- Cadence: teaser prelaunch; flagship webinar launch; 2 videos/month post-launch
- KPI: registrations, attendance, retention, post-event meetings

## Partnerships/Communities
- Cadence: outreach prelaunch; launch announcements; biweekly co-marketing
- KPI: referral quality, partner-sourced opportunities

## Sales Enablement
- Cadence: weekly prelaunch; daily short launch standups; weekly post-launch review
- Outputs: scripts, battlecards, objection map, proof sheet
- KPI: rep readiness + stage conversion trend
