## 4) Claim Governance (Mandatory)
All external copy must use claim tags:
- **Verified** (evidence linked)
- **Estimate** (explicitly directional)
- **Future** (roadmap only)
- **Unverified** (do not publish)

**Hard rules:**
- No unverified numeric claims in public assets.
- No compliance/security superlatives without explicit owner/legal sign-off.
- Every externally used asset gets a “Last verified” date + owner.

## 7) Campaign Breakdown by Platform
## LinkedIn
- Objective: Credibility + inbound demo demand
- Audience: PM operators, founders, ops leads
- Cadence: 3/wk prelaunch; daily launch week; 2–3/wk post-launch
- Content: workflow carousels, operator POV, short demos
- KPIs: CTR, qualified inbound demos, engagement quality
- Owner: PMM + Content Lead

## X/Twitter
- Objective: Real-time awareness + conversation
- Cadence: 1 thread/wk + short posts prelaunch; 2/day launch; daily weekdays post-launch
- Content: build updates, short tactical threads, webinar clips
- KPIs: link clicks, qualified replies, webinar registrations
- Owner: Social Lead

## Email
- Objective: Lead nurture to booked meetings
- Cadence: weekly prelaunch; 2 sends launch week; weekly post-launch + triggers
- Content: launch narrative, role use-cases, objection handling
- KPIs: open trend, CTR, meeting-booked conversion
- Owner: Lifecycle Marketing

## Website/SEO
- Objective: Capture intent + convert site traffic
- Cadence: 2 landing pages + 2 SEO pieces prelaunch; launch hero/page on go-live; 1–2 SEO pieces/wk post-launch
- Content: core solution pages, FAQ, role pages, webinar replay
- KPIs: demo conversion rate, organic traffic trend, engagement
- Owner: Growth + SEO/Web

## YouTube/Webinar
- Objective: Visual proof + shorter sales cycle
- Cadence: teaser prelaunch; flagship webinar launch; 2 videos/month post-launch
- Content: walkthroughs, role deep-dives, Q&A clips
- KPIs: registrations, attendance, watch retention, post-event meetings
- Owner: PMM + Solutions Engineer

## Communities/Partnerships
- Objective: Third-party trust and referrals
- Cadence: outreach prelaunch; 2 launch announcements; biweekly co-marketing post-launch
- Content: AMAs, partner kits, co-branded sessions
- KPIs: referral traffic quality, partner-sourced opportunities
- Owner: Partnerships

## Sales Enablement
- Objective: Message consistency in pipeline conversion
- Cadence: weekly prelaunch; daily short launch standup; weekly post-launch review
- Content: battlecards, scripts, objection sheet, proof map
- KPIs: rep readiness, stage conversion, win/loss reasons
- Owner: Sales Enablement + PMM
