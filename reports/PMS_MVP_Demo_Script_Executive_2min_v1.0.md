# PMS MVP Demo Script — Executive (2-Minute Version)

**Audience:** executive stakeholders, partners, investors  
**Length:** ~2 minutes

---

## 1) Opening (15 sec)

"In two minutes, I’ll show you what matters most: this PMS runs the full property operations lifecycle in one system — leasing, payments, maintenance, inspections, owner visibility, and AI-readiness controls."

---

## 2) Business Flow (45 sec)

"From applicant to active tenant, the workflow is connected end-to-end. Application review, lease onboarding, and payment enablement happen without handoff friction. That reduces turnaround time and operational overhead for PM teams."

"On payments, we support both digital and real-world offline operations — including manual cash/check/money-order entry and manual charges — with auditable reverse/void actions."

---

## 3) Operations + Service Quality (35 sec)

"Maintenance requests flow from tenant submission into PM triage with context preserved, including photos and request history. Inspections are structured and repeatable across move-in, routine, and move-out scenarios."

"AI assists with estimate/work-plan generation, but with history and traceability so teams can validate and explain decisions."

---

## 4) Owner + AI Strategy (20 sec)

"Owners get transparent visibility into performance and maintenance status without manual reporting burden on PM staff."

"And our AI rollout is evidence-based: we measure data-readiness before claiming model reliability."

---

## 5) Close (10 sec)

"Bottom line: this platform is execution-first, auditable, and demo-proven — with practical AI adoption layered on top of strong operational fundamentals."
